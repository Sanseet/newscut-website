Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

1. Who we are

NewsCut ("we", "our", "us") operates news.hapliq.com and provides short-form news summaries. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

When you visit NewsCut, we may collect: (a) basic usage data such as pages viewed, language preference, and device/browser type; (b) anonymous analytics to understand traffic patterns; and (c) any information you voluntarily provide (for example, if you contact us by email).

3. How we use information

We use information to operate and improve NewsCut, deliver content in your preferred language, monitor performance and security, and respond to messages you send us. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Cookies & local storage

We use a small number of cookies and local-storage entries — for example, to remember your language choice. You can clear these at any time from your browser settings.

5. Third-party services

NewsCut is hosted on Vercel and routed via Cloudflare. These providers process basic request data (such as IP address) as part of delivering the site. If we add analytics or other services later, we will update this policy.

6. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. See the "Data Deletion" section below for how to make a request.

7. Children

NewsCut is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email newscut@hapliq.com.

Data Deletion

Last updated: 18 May 2026

Request the deletion of your data

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data NewsCut holds about you. We honor verified requests within 30 days.

How to request

Send an email to newscut@hapliq.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • The email address or identifier associated with your data
  • A short description of what you want deleted (e.g. "all data")
  • Any context that helps us verify the request is yours

What happens next

We will confirm receipt within 3 business days, verify your identity, and then permanently delete your data from our active systems. Backups containing the data are cycled out within 90 days. Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot be tied back to you may be retained.
